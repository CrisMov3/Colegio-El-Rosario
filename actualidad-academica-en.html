Preschool

Starting the path to knowledge

Our preschool program offers a safe, warm and stimulating environment where children develop their cognitive, communicative, bodily, affective and social dimensions through play, exploration and meaningful learning.

Program Characteristics:

  • Grades: Transition (5 years) and Pre-K (4 years)
  • Schedule: 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM
  • Pedagogical approach: Playful and meaningful
  • Development areas: Cognitive, communicative, bodily, affective and social
  • Early English: Early immersion in the language
  • Artistic activities: Music, art and creative expression
  • Physical education: Motor development and playful activities

Institutional values in preschool

At this fundamental stage, we foster respect, responsibility, solidarity and love as the foundation for forming whole and happy human beings.

Primary School

Building solid foundations

In elementary school, our focus centers on strengthening fundamental competencies in reading, writing and logical-mathematical thinking, while experiencing institutional values in every educational action. We prepare reflective, critical and creative students.

Curriculum Plan:

  • Grades: 1st to 5th
  • Schedule: 12:30 PM to 5:30 PM
  • Main subjects: Spanish, Mathematics, Natural Sciences, Social Sciences
  • English: Progressive development of communicative competencies
  • Integral formation: Ethics, religious education, physical education, artistic education
  • Technology: Introduction to digital tools and robotics
  • Transverse projects: Environmental education and responsible resource use

Active methodology

We use innovative pedagogical strategies that include collaborative learning, integrative projects and continuous assessment to ensure meaningful learning.

High School

Toward academic and human excellence

We form competent, critical and reflective young people capable of taking on the challenges of the contemporary world. Our curriculum deepens in sciences and humanities, preparing students for higher education and responsible citizenship.

Academic Information:

  • Grades: 6th to 11th
  • Schedule: 6:30 AM to 1:00 PM
  • Academic emphasis: Sciences, Humanities and English
  • In-depth subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Calculus, Philosophy
  • Programming: Introduction to programming and web development
  • ICFES preparation: Intensive in grades 10th and 11th
  • Entrepreneurship: Business project as elective subject
  • Languages: Advanced English and elective second language

Institutional horizon

We seek that our graduates be ethical leaders, competent and committed to the sustainable development of society, capable of transforming their reality from critical reflection and responsible action.
