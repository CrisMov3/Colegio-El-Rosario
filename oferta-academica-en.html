Academic Offer

Learn about the educational levels offered by El Rosario School of Itagüí within the framework of its institutional project and current regulations.

📘 Educational Offer

As a formal education project, the scope of the offer is broad, since it covers nearly all school age ranges until students reach adulthood and move on to their higher education studies.

El Rosario School of Itagüí offers the following options within the framework of its institutional plan and legal regulations:

  • a) Preschool: pre-kindergarten, kindergarten, and transition. These grades may be offered in integrated groups depending on each year's enrollment composition, always with a well-defined academic integration plan and differentiated methodologies. The minimum age for admission to the integrated cycle is three and a half years.
  • b) Elementary Education: grades 1st to 5th. The minimum age for enrollment in first grade is six years old.
  • c) Lower Secondary Education: grades 6th to 9th.
  • d) Upper Secondary Education: grades 10th and 11th.

With the appropriate promotion criteria established at each level and cycle, the degree is awarded under the modality of Academic High School Diploma.
